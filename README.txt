## Advanced Search Disabler

With the Advanced Search Disabler, you can set specific settings for your website’s search functionality.

You can disable search for:

	* The entire website
	* Users who are not logged in
	* Or exclude specific posts

It’s all easy to configure, and you can find the settings under the menu “Settings -> Advanced Search Disabler.”

Visit us at [LetoWP](https://letowp.dev/?utm_source=wordpress.org&utm_medium=advanced-search-disabler)
